About Samuel O. Damilare

Hello, My name is Samuel Damilare but friends call me Dami or D. I am a graduate of Computer Science from Lagos State University, Lagos, Nigeria. I am currently running my Masters degree in Computer Science from the University of Lagos, Lagos

I was born and bred in Lagos State and I have grown fond of it over the years. In recent times, the congestion and traffic in the state gives a lot to worry about, but it is still one of the best state in the country. Little wonder it's called the centre of excellence.

I like to fill up my life with learning new things, playing the keyboard, reading (especially stories for teens e.g Sweet Sixteen by Bolaji Abdullahi) as well as teaching. How I love teaching especially when it comes to educating young ones.

This place serves as a portfolio site that shows the things I'm working on and things I've done. This site will always be progresive and perhaps often time change as I work on new things, I will try to upload on the site. Feel free to explore the site.

Thank you for visiting !
